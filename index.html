About Prisci Constructions

Two decades of
engineering excellence.

ISO Certified Govt. Approved 250+ Projects

Founded in 1999, Prisci Constructions is a multidisciplinary engineering and construction firm specializing in structural rehabilitation, waterproofing, civil infrastructure, architecture, and interior design.

With over two decades of field experience, we have successfully delivered projects across government, industrial, commercial, and residential sectors. Our expertise extends from diagnosing complex building failures to delivering engineered repair, protection, and design solutions that enhance the life and performance of structures.

Today, Prisci serves clients across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, and Maharashtra with a commitment to quality, technical excellence, and long-term value.
